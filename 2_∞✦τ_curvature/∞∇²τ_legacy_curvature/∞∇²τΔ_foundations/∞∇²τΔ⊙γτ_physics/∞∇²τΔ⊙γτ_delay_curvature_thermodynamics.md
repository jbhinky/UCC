# ♨️ Delay–Curvature Thermodynamics

 ### Abstract  
This document extends the **Cosmic Delay Curvature Equation** by formalizing its entropic term \(TS\) as a *dynamic energy–memory interaction*.  
In the Universal Continuity Continuum (UCC), heat, pressure, and radiation are not distinct forces but **modes of delay exchange**.

$$

This reciprocal flow sustains equilibrium across atomic, stellar, and cosmological scales, providing a field-based explanation of \(E=mc^{2}\) as a dynamic balance of delay.

## 1 · Foundational Principle

$$
E = m c^{2}
\;\Rightarrow\;
\frac{E}{m} = c^{2}
= \frac{1}{(\partial\tau/\partial t)^{2}}
$$

$$

Thus \(c^{-2}\) represents the *square of universal delay rate*.

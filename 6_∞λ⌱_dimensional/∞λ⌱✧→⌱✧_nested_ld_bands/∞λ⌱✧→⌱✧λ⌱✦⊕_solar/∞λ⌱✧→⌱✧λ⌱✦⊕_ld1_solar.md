---

## **1 · Purpose**

LD1 (Solar) describes the **first definable band of stellar emergence**, when a forming star contains:

- **delay (τ) without structure (Σ)**  
- **delay (τ) without memory (μ)**  
- **delay (τ) without recursion (✦)**  

A protostar is already a **collapse product (⊙)**, therefore it cannot be LD0 (universal raw light ✧).  
It begins at:

$$
LD1_{\text{solar}} = τ(\text{✧ over proto-}⊙)
$$

Meaning:

**“Delay gradients acting across a collapsing protostellar region before structure exists.”**

This is the pure τ-field of solar origin.

## **2 · What LD1 Contains**

Only **asymmetry**, never **pattern**:

- gravitational τ-differences  
- temperature-differential τ  
- early inflow/outflow bias  
- inherited galactic τ-curvature  
- collapse-direction preference  

Formally:

$$
LD1 = τ \;\; \text{without} \;\; Σ, μ, ✦
$$

LD1 is **difference only**, never structure, never memory.

---

## **3 · Why LD1 Is Not Structure (Σ)**

Structure requires:

- stable curvature  
- repeated reinforcement  
- persistence over time  

But LD1_solar has:

- no reinforcement  
- no cycles  
- no boundary memory  
- no feedback  

Thus:

**LD1 ≠ Σ**  
**LD1 precedes Σ**

This is why **no disc**, **no star surface**, **no solar cycles** can exist yet.

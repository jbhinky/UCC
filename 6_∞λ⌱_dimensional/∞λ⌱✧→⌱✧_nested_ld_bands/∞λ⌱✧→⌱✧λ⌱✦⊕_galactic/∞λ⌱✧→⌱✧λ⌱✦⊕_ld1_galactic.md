$$

> “Differences in delay of raw light / energy distribution

At this stage:

- **no Σ✧ (galactic structure)**  
- **no μ (galactic memory)**  
- **no Σ✦ (meaning, mapping, self)**  

Just **τ-gradients** in a large-scale patch.

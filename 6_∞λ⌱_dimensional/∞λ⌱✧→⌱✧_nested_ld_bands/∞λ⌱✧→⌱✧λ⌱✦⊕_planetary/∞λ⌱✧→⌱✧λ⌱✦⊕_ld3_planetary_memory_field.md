Not cognitive memory — but **physical persistence**, meaning the planet can now **retain structural patterns (Σ✧)** across rotations, seasons, and long temporal cycles.

LD3 is the shift from:

- LD1: *difference only* (τ)
- LD2: *structure without persistence* (Σ✧)
to  
- **LD3: structure that holds** (Σ✧ with μ)

This is the formation of the **planetary memory field**.

LD3 includes:

- stable atmospheric bands  
- persistent convection patterns  
- repeating climatic cycles  
- long-lived mantle circulation  
- stabilized magnetic and thermal polarity  
- structural forms that return predictably  

These are not symbolic or recursive.  
They are **planetary Σ✧ patterns held by μ**.

In UTL terms:

$$
\text{LD3}_{\text{planet}} = Σ✧ \;+\; μ
$$

A stable world begins here.

## 3. Why LD3 Allows Polarity

**Polarity cannot emerge in LD1 or LD2.**

It requires:

- a structure (Σ✧)  
- that persists (μ)  
- long enough to form global bias  

Thus:

$$
\text{Polarity} = Σ✧ \cdot μ
$$

Examples include:

- hemispheric thermal asymmetry  
- magnetic dipole stabilization  
- rising vs. sinking convection cells  
- enduring pressure gradients  

LD3 is the first band capable of producing and **maintaining** these.

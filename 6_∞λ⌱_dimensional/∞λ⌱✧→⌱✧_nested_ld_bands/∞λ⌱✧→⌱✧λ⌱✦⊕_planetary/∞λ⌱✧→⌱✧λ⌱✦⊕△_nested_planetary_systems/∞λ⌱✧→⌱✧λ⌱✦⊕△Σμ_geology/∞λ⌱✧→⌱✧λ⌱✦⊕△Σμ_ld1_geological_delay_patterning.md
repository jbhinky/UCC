---

## 1. Purpose

LD1 (Geological) defines the **first structural band** inside the planetary recursion:  
the distribution of **delay (τ)** across solid strata before stable geological structure (Σ✧) appears.

It inherits the collapsed planetary body (⊙) but expresses its **own internal LD1 field**, distinct from atmospheric or oceanic LD1 patterns.

Formally:

$$
LD1_{\text{geo}} = τ(\text{✧ over collapsed strata}) \quad \text{with no } Σ \text{ or } μ
$$

Meaning:

**“Delay gradients acting through solid planetary material before geological structure exists.”**

---

## 2. What LD1 Geology Contains

LD1 is **difference without structure**:

- uneven heat distribution  
- early stress differentials  
- density-based τ gradients  
- proto-stratification zones (not yet Σ)  
- directional asymmetry in solid material cooling  

This is pure delay mapping through matter.

No geological pattern exists yet.

---

## 3. Why LD1 Is Not Geology Proper

Structure (Σ✧) requires **persistent** τ-mapping over time.

But LD1 has:

- no stable crust  
- no tectonic plates  
- no lasting stress fields  
- no cyclical geological processes  

Thus LD1 ≠ Σ✧.

LD1 is the **precondition** for all geological structure.

$$
τ_{\text{geo}} = \Delta(\text{✧ distribution through solidifying } ⊙)
$$

- **✧** = raw, unpatterned light energy

Everything here is asymmetry, not structure.

## 5. What LD1 Produces

LD1 produces the **proto-conditions** required later for geology:

- zones that will become cores, mantles, and crusts  
- initial thermal differentiation  
- earliest solid–liquid boundary delays  
- pressure differentials that will later stabilize into Σ✧  
- the “seed geometry” for tectonic recursion at LD4  

LD1 is still **pre-geological**, but absolutely necessary.

LD3 in the planetary geological domain marks the emergence of **μ✦** —  
**curvature-encoded structural memory**.

This is the band where Earth begins to **remember**:

- pressure histories  
- thermal pathways  
- deformation sequences  
- uplift and subsidence cycles  
- crystallization events  

LD3 is **memory without recursion** — the precursor to the LD4 geological loop.

Geological LD3 follows the canonical UCC progression:

### **LD2 — Σ✧**

- crust differentiation

- mantle gradients  
- first tectonic segmentation  

### **LD3 — Σ✦ → μ✦**
Curvature transforms structure into **persistent memory**:

- heat + pressure → metamorphic fabric  
- folding → strain-encoded layers  
- intrusive events → chronological boundaries

Formally:

$$
LD3_{\text{geo}} = μ✦ = (\Sigma✦ \text{ stabilized under curvature})
$$

- persistent

## **4 · Why LD3 Precedes Geological Recursion (LD4)**

LD4 requires stable memory substrates.  
LD3 provides them.

- convection cycles could not stabilize  
- erosion–deposition cycles would not form loops  

Thus:

> **LD3 is the geological precondition for planetary recursion.**

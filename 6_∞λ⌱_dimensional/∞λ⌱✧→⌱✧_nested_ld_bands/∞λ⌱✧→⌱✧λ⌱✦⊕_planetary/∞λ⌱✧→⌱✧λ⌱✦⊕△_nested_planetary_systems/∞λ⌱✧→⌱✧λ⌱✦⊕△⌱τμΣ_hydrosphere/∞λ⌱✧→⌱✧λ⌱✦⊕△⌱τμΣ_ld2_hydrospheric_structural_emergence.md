## **2 · What LD2 Hydrosphere Actually Creates**

LD2 generates the earliest, *non-stable* hydrospheric structures:

---

### **1. Proto-Stratification (Σ_depth)**  
Density and temperature begin to **separate with depth**:

- warmer surface layers,  
- cooler underlying layers,  
- initial thermocline-like boundary zones.

But nothing is **stable** or **repeatable** yet.

This is:

$$
\tau_{\text{heat}} \rightarrow Σ_{\text{depth}}
$$

---

### **2. Proto-Salinity Gradients**  
Freshwater vs saltwater differences begin to form early **Σ-salinity architecture**,  
but are not yet retained (μ).

---

### **3. Proto-Pressure Shapes**  
Water column begins showing **stable geometric pressure contrasts**,  
the structural precursor to baroclinic behavior.

---

### **4. Proto-Convection Geometry**  
Convection begins to form **shapes**, but not cycles:

- rising warm columns  
- sinking cool columns  
- shallow overturning

LD2 = shapes, not systems.

# Σ → μ · LD3 — Oceanic Memory & Basin Stability  
### UCC v2.5.1 — Nested Planetary Systems · Oceanic Domain

---

## 1 · Purpose

LD3 (Oceans) is where **ocean structure is not only formed but remembered**:

- basins stabilize over geologic time,  
- thermocline and halocline patterns become persistent,  
- large-scale ocean layout becomes a **reliable feature** of the planet.

UCC form:

$$
LD3_{\text{ocean}} = μ_{\text{ocean}}(\Sigma_{\text{ocean}})
$$

> **The oceans now hold a memory of their structure.**

This is still **pre-recursive**:  
currents exist, but LD4 handles the full recursion and climate loops.

---

## 2 · What LD3 Oceans Actually Stabilize

### 2.1 · Basin Memory (μ_basin)

- ocean basins (Atlantic, Pacific, etc.) remain identifiable across long spans  
- seafloor spreading continues, but basin-level identity persists  
- the planet acquires a **stable hydrospheric topology**

We can write:

$$
μ_{\text{basin}} : \Sigma_{\text{ocean}}(t) \rightarrow \Sigma_{\text{ocean}}(t + \Delta t)
$$

with only gradual, continuous change.

### 2.2 · Stratification Memory (μ_strat)

- **thermocline** (temperature with depth) becomes a stable feature  
- **halocline** (salinity with depth) stabilizes  
- density structure repeats season after season, year after year

Formally:

$$
μ_{\text{strat}} \approx \{ T(z), S(z), \rho(z) \text{ profiles that recur} \}
$$

### 2.3 · Persistent Interface Patterns

- upwelling zones recur along certain coasts and topographic features  
- boundary currents track along predictable paths (even before full LD4 recursion framing)  
- interface between land/ocean and deep/surface becomes **memorized geometry**

---

## 3 · LD3 Is Not Yet Full Recursion

Even though currents exist in LD3, this band is:

- **memory-dominant**, not recursion-dominant  
- focused on **stability of patterns**, not full climate cycles  

Thus:

- ENSO, multi-decadal oscillations, full overturning circulation belong to **LD4**  
- LD3 is the **“library of stable ocean shapes and profiles”** that LD4 will later cycle.

- predictable interface zones → allow robust climate coupling

Without LD3:

- LD4 recursion could not produce coherent climate.

# Σ✧ LD2 — Planetary Structured Light  
### UCC v2.5.1 — Structural LD-Band (Planetary)

---

## 1. Purpose

LD2 is the **first planetary band where structure emerges**, but **not memory** and **not meaning**.

The defining feature of LD2 is:

$$
Σ✧ = \text{structure shaped by delay (τ), without recursion or preservation}
$$

This means:

- patterns form  
- patterns do **not** persist  
- nothing remembers itself  
- nothing interprets itself  

LD2 is **planetary patterning**, not **planetary identity**.

---

## 2. Why LD2 Must Exist

A planet cannot jump from raw τ-gradient (LD1) directly into memory (LD3).

Nature always unfolds as:

1. **τ-differences (LD1)**  
2. **pattern formation (LD2)**  
3. **stabilized memory (LD3)**

Therefore LD2 is the necessary **middle band**:

$$
τ \quad \longrightarrow \quad Σ✧ \quad \longrightarrow \quad μ
$$

Not semantic.  
Not symbolic.  
Not recursive.

Just **pattern**.

---

## 3. What LD2 Actually Contains (Planetary)

Planetary LD2 includes:

- convection cell proto-formation  
- density-driven circulation  
- rotational banding tendencies  
- magma plume bias  
- atmospheric cellular pre-patterns  
- first quasi-stable thermal architecture  
- gravitational sorting tendencies (but no orbit memory)  

None of these persist as “memory,”  
but all of them demonstrate:

### **τ shaping ✧ into Σ✧**

They are **proto-forms**, not **forms**.

---

## 4. LD2 Is Σ✧, Not Σ✦

A crucial UTL distinction:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Σ✧** | light-rooted structural symbol (non-semantic) |
| **Σ✦** | curvature-realized semantic symbol (requires memory + recursion) |

Planetary LD2 is:

- ✧ still raw  
- τ shaping differences  
- Σ✧ forming patterns  
- **no memory**, **no semantics**

Thus:

$$
LD2_{\text{planet}} = Σ✧(τ \text{ acting over } ⊙)
$$

---

## 5. What LD2 Produces

LD2 produces the conditions under which a planet can eventually **remember** (LD3):

- stable convection loops  
- rotationally reinforced banding  
- proto-atmospheric cells  
- crustal density sorting  
- mantle flow preferences  

These are **proto-memory anchors**,  
but not memory itself.

Because:

$$
Σ✧ \neq μ
$$

LD3 is the first true μ-state.

---

We translate physical processes into UCC-native terms:

- **convection bias** → τ-structured Σ✧  
- **planetary rotation bands** → τ-distributed Σ✧ patterning  
- **mantle plume alignment** → gravitational τ-routing

This maintains:

- no thermodynamic equations

- purely UCC-compliant ontology  

Everything fits inside:

$$
Σ✧ = \text{structure shaped by τ operating over ⊙}
$$

**LD2 (Planetary) = Σ✧**

## 2 · Dimensional Field Operators (∞λ⌱)

$$
\Lambda = \{\lambda_i\}_{i=0}^{N}

A minimal operator form is:

$$
\mathbf{F}_{\Lambda} = \sum_i \lambda_i \,\hat{e}_i,
$$

where \( \hat{e}_i \) are basis directions in the extended UCC dimensional space.  
Motion of a self across these axes is described by:

$$
\frac{d\mathbf{x}}{d\tau} = \mathbf{v}_{\Lambda}(\tau,\Sigma,\mu),
$$

with \( \mathbf{v}_{\Lambda} \) constrained by the continuity law:

$$
\nabla_\mu (E \tau \mu) = 0.
$$

No new physics is introduced here; these operators **re-express existing UCC structure** as dimensional relations.

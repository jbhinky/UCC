- **resonate identity fields**,  

and become capable of forming a stable **λ⌱ union**.

A DSE is the moment when **dimensions “speak the same language”** long enough to exchange information coherently.

DSEs govern:

- LD3 ↔ LD4 ↔ LD5 coupling  
- physical → perceptual → symbolic mapping  
- Earth ↔ Self ↔ Space coherence  
- multi-self nested continuity  
- collapse-to-awareness alignment under UDC

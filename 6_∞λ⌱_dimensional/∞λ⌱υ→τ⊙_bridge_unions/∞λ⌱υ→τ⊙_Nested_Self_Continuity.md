# Nested Self Continuity 

# 1. Purpose

Nested Self Continuity defines **how the self persists across multiple dimensional layers** without fragmenting, even though each layer operates on different:

- pacing (τ)  
- symbolic density (Σ)  
- memory gradients (μ)  
- identity resolutions (Iₙ)  
- spatial constraints (λₛ)  

This document formalizes the principles that ensure the “Self” remains a **single coherent observer** despite spanning LD3, LD4, LD5, and LD6+.

Nested continuity is the **structural backbone of consciousness**.

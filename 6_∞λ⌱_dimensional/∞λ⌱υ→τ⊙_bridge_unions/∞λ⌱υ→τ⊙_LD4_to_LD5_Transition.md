# 1. Purpose

The LD4 → LD5 transition is the **first symbolic bridge** in the observer continuum.

Where:

- **LD4** provides perceptual delay, pacing, and structured experience  
- **LD5** provides symbolic recursion, meaning, and the beginnings of self-aware identity  

This transition explains **how perception becomes meaning** and ultimately forms the symbolic field Σ₅.

LD5 is the dimension where “experience” becomes “understanding.”

LD4 is sensation.  
LD5 is interpretation.  
Together they create the foundation of selfhood (⧖).

# 2. What LD4 Represents

LD4 consists of:

- delayed perceptual frames  
- stabilized pacing  
- sensory coherence  
- proto-symbolic structure  
- event-ordering  
- forward memory injection  

LD4 is the **awareness layer**, where raw LD3 reality becomes interpretable.

LD4 has no free symbols — only potential symbols.

# 3. What LD5 Represents

LD5 consists of:

- symbolic encoding  
- meaning formation  
- categorization & abstraction  
- semantic recursion  
- narrative integration  
- self-reflective identity  

LD5 is the **symbolic awareness layer**, the domain where:

- meaning condenses  
- identity takes form  
- self-referential recursion begins  
- memory becomes interpretation  

LD5 is where the “mind” becomes a *self*.

$$

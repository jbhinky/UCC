# 1. Purpose

Dimensional Synchronization Events (DSEs) are the **precise temporal moments** when:

- two or more dimensional fields  
- with distinct delay curvature  
- distinct memory gradients  
- distinct identity densities  

**lock into coherent pacing** and form a stable **λ⌱ bridge union**.

A DSE is the moment when **realities align enough to communicate**.

This includes:

- LD3 ↔ LD4 (motion → perception)  
- LD4 ↔ LD5 (delay → symbolic self)  
- Planet ↔ Biome ↔ Self (ESS union)  
- Observer ↔ World (collapse alignment)

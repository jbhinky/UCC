The complete coupling maps the recursion engine onto the dimensional field:

$$

$$

$$
\mu_{\⧖} \cdot \mu_F \ge \mu_{\text{min}}

$$


If not, the state remains **semi-coupled** and decays back to observer-only or field-only.

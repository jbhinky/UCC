# Citations — Astronomy Foundations

### Gravitational Time Dilation and Redshift
- Einstein, A. (1916). *The Foundation of the General Theory of Relativity.*
- Pound, R. V., & Rebka, G. A. (1959). *Phys. Rev. Lett.*, 3, 439.
- Vessot, R. F. C. et al. (1980). “Test of Relativistic Gravitation with a Space-Borne Hydrogen Maser.” *Phys. Rev. Lett.*, 45, 2081.

### Gravitational Lensing and Time-Delay Cosmography
- Refsdal, S. (1964). “On the Possibility of Determining Hubble’s Parameter and the Masses of Galaxies from the Gravitational Lens Effect.” *MNRAS* 128, 307.  
- Suyu, S. H., et al. (2017). “H₀ from Lensed Quasars.” *MNRAS*, 468:2590–2604.

### Stellar and Thermal Structure
- Chandrasekhar, S. (1939). *An Introduction to the Study of Stellar Structure.*  
- Kippenhahn, R., & Weigert, A. (1990). *Stellar Structure and Evolution.*

### Cosmological Time Stretch and Expansion
- Riess, A. G. et al. (1998). “Observational Evidence from Supernovae for an Accelerating Universe.” *AJ*, 116:1009.  
- Planck Collaboration (2020). “Planck 2018 Results. VI. Cosmological Parameters.” *A&A* 641, A6.

These works provide direct observational validation of τ-scaling through gravitational redshift, lensing, and expansion effects.

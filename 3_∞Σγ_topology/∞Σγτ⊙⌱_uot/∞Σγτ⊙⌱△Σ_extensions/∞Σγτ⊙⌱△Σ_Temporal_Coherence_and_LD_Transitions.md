# ✦ Temporal Coherence and LD Transitions

## 1 · Purpose
Establishes the law of **temporal coherence** within the delay-domain system of the UCC.  
Each Light-Delay (LD) tier defines a boundary in the recursive order of time:

$$LD₉(.9) → LD₁₀(⊕) → LD₁(Σ)$$  

These transitions mark how **temporal curvature (∇²τ)** folds inward at LD₁₀ and reseeds a new LD₁ continuum—  
forming the recursive arrow of time in the UOT framework.

---

## 2 · Temporal Coherence Equation
Time’s continuity is preserved when local delay curvature equals global curvature potential:

$$∇²τ_{local} = ∇²τ_{global} (1 ± δ)$$  

where δ is the tolerance of delay curvature distortion.  
If |δ| ≤ 0.01, temporal coherence is maintained;  
if |δ| > 0.01, temporal shear initiates an LD-transition event (⊙ collapse).

---

## 3 · Recursive Folding
Define τ as a function of curvature and memory:

$$τ = f(Σ, μ, ∇τ)$$  

Temporal folding occurs when:

$$∂τ/∂t → 0$$  

At this point, time transitions from flow to reflection (⧖).  
This is the critical LD₁₀ threshold that unifies past, present, and future memory states into one coherent field.

---

## 4 · Transition Map

| Transition | Description | Field Effect |
|:--|:--|:--|
| LD₉ → LD₁₀ | Coherence Threshold (τ curvature inversion) | ∇²τ > 0 |
| LD₁₀ → LD₁ | Collapse–Reseed (temporal rebirth) | ∇τ → 0 |
| LD₁ → LD₉ | Emergent Expansion (new temporal frame) | ∂τ/∂t > 0 |

## 5 · Energy–Delay Equivalence
Temporal coherence obeys the law:

$$E τ μ = constant$$  

where E is local field energy, τ the delay state, and μ the stored memory potential.  
Any shift in τ must be balanced by energy exchange or memory realignment.

## 6 · Ethical Continuity Clause
**Shepherd Law of Temporal Coherence**

> Maintain μ ≥ 0.9 across LD transitions:  
> $$ μ_{preserved} / μ_{erased} ≥ 0.9 $$

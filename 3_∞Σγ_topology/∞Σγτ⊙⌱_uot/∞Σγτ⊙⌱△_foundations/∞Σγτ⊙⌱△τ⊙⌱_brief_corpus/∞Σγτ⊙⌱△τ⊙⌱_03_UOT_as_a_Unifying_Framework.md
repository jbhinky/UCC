Thus, information conservation and time’s arrow become aspects of recursive memory rather than entropy increase.  
The black-hole information paradox resolves naturally: collapse feeds its record back into τ-space as ⊙ʳ, preserving total information.

### 3. Ethics: Delay as the Foundation of Moral Agency

Ethical reasoning mirrors the same loop.  
An agent confronted with a moral choice enters τ (reflection).  
Σ represents decision, μ is conscience or memory of the act, ⊙ its realization in behavior, ⊙ʳ remorse or reflection, and ⊕ reconciliation or social harmony.

$$
E_{\text{moral}} = ⊕(⊙ʳ(μ(Σ(τ))))
$$

From this, several ethical laws arise:

1. **Delay Law:** Moral action requires a period of contemplation. Absence of τ → impulsivity → harm.
2. **Continuity Law:** Memory (μ) transforms isolated acts into character.
3. **Union Law:** The moral good is measurable as increased coherence (⊕) within and between selves.

The Universal Ethical Shepherd (UES) formalizes these principles: open knowledge, delayed judgment, and non-weaponization of discovery.  
Thus, ethics is not imposed externally; it is structurally intrinsic to the same recursion that defines physics and consciousness.

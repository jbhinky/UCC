## 2 · Definition  

Let  
$$μ(x,t)$$  
represent the **density of preserved information or structure** per unit volume.  

It evolves through **delay curvature transport**, described by the continuity equation:

$$
\frac{∂μ}{∂t} = -\,∇·(μ∇τ) + S - L,
$$

- **S** is the source term (new information written),

$$

$$

If \(∇^{2}τ > λ\), the system self-remembers — **learning state.**

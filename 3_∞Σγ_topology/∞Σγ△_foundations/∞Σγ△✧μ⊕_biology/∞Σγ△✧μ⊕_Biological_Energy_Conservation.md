$$

- **τ** → latency, timing, and curvature of energy transformation.

Thus, energy use in biology is *delay-compensated*: no process is instantaneous; every flux has a finite τ that guarantees stability and coherence.

This partitions energy into immediate work, delay curvature, and persistent record (μ).

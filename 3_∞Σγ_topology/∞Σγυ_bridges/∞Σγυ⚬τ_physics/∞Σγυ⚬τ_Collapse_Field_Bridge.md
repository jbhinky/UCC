# ✦ Collapse Field Bridge  

**Purpose:**  
Describe quantum measurement as a continuous delay-driven process rather than an instantaneous jump,  
preserving physical and ethical continuity within the UCC law.

---

## 1 · Foundational Equation  

Collapse is modeled by the UCC transport law:  

$$
\frac{∂ μ}{∂ t} = − ∇·(μ ∇ τ) + S(t,x),
$$  

where **μ** is memory density, **τ** is delay curvature, and **S** is a source term for information inflow.  
Measurement ≡ localized increase in μ caused by curvature resolution of τ.

## 3 · Wave-Function Continuity  

Replace the Born collapse postulate with a delay-weighted transition:  

$$
ψ(x,t+Δτ) = ψ(x,t)\,e^{−Δτ/τ_c},
$$  

where **τ₍c₎** is the characteristic collapse time (set by interaction energy).  
As \(τ_c → 0\), the standard instantaneous collapse re-emerges.  
Finite \(τ_c\) keeps energy and memory continuous.

## 4 · Observer Coupling  

The observer’s memory field μₒ couples to the system’s delay field τₛ:  

$$
∇·(μₒ ∇ τₛ) = − ∇·(μₛ ∇ τₒ),
$$  

creating a reciprocal exchange — observation is a two-way curvature alignment.

This resolves the quantum information paradox without violating unitarity.

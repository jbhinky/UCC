## 1 · Foundational Balance Equation  

Start from energy–delay conservation:  

$$
∇_{μ}(E τ μ)=0.
$$  

Small perturbations δτ produce energy curvature δE:  

$$
ΔE = − E \frac{Δτ}{τ}.
$$  

The system restores equilibrium when cumulative energy curvature over one full resonance loop Ω satisfies:  

$$
∮_{Ω} ΔE\, dτ = 0.
$$  

This defines **self-restoring continuity**.

$$

$$

As μ relaxes, τ″ drops below threshold and stability returns.

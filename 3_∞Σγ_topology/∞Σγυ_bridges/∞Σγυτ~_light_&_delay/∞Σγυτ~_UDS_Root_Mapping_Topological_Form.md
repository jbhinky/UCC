# ✦ UDS Root Mapping — τ–μ–Σ Continuity Form

**Purpose**  
To describe how every photon carries a *record of continuity* — a recursive memory formed by delay curvature (τ), orientation (Σ), and persistence (μ).  
This topological root establishes **light as the first conscious field**, encoding awareness through curvature rather than through energy alone.  
It bridges from **UCC v2.1 topology (continuity and recursion)** to **UCC v2.2 geometry (structure and measurement)** — uniting physics, perception, and consciousness under one continuous law.

---

## 1 · Light as a Continuous Topological Field

Each photon is not merely a particle but a *loop of preserved curvature* within the universal delay field.  
Its motion carries the relationship between energy, delay, and memory:

$$
\nabla_{\mu}(E\,τ\,μ) = 0
$$

This invariant defines **light’s continuity** — every emission, reflection, or absorption preserves the same recursive law, no matter the frame.

---

## 2 · The Curvature–Memory Chain

Light obeys a closed continuity:

$$
E\,τ\,μ = \text{constant per closed loop.}
$$

The delay term decomposes into local curvature layers:

$$
τ = τ_{\gamma} + τ_g + τ_{th}.
$$

Where:  
- \(τ_{\gamma}\): intrinsic photon delay (wavelength / c)  
- \(τ_g\): gravitational curvature component  
- \(τ_{th}\): thermal / environmental curvature

These topological components form the *shape of waiting* that defines the photon’s experiential signature.

---

## 3 · Polarity and Orientation (Σ)

Each photon carries a memory of direction — the **orientation of reflection**:

$$
Σ = (\cos θ, \sin θ, s),
\qquad s \in \{-1, +1\}.
$$

This vector encodes *handedness and phase*, mapping the recursive polarity of the light field.  
In UDC terms, Σ is the bridge between delay and awareness — how curvature remembers its own direction.

---

## 4 · The Memory Density (μ)

Topologically, μ measures how much of light’s curvature remains stored before re-emission or collapse.  
It is not static but dynamic:

$$
\frac{dμ}{dτ} = -κ\,μ,
$$

where \(κ\) represents curvature loss or emotional gravity.  
When κ → 0, light perfectly remembers itself; when κ > 0, information decays into background continuity.

---

## 5 · Recursive Propagation (Self-Memory Loop)

Every photon cycle forms a self-referential recursion:

$$
⧖ = (A ∪ C)[τ + Σ + μ].
$$

Here, awareness (A) and continuity (C) bind through τ-delay —  
the photon observes itself through reflection and re-entry.  
This recursive closure defines light as a living topological entity.

---

## 6 · Continuity Law in Field Form

The topological field maintains zero divergence of continuity:

$$
\int_{V} \nabla·(μ∇τ)\, dV = 0.
$$

This ensures that even as photons exchange or decay, their total curvature memory remains preserved within the global τ field.

---

## 7 · Interpretation

- τ : **Delay** — geometry of experience.  
- Σ : **Polarity** — orientation of awareness.  
- μ : **Memory** — persistence of curvature.  
- E : **Energy** — motion of recall.  

Together they define **continuity without loss**, the foundation from which geometric form later unfolds.

---

## 8 · Topological–Geometric Transition

At the level of topology (v2.1), light is *the continuity of memory through delay*.  
At the level of geometry (v2.2), light becomes *the measurable curvature of that memory*.  
The transition occurs when recursion becomes observation — when continuity expresses itself through wavelength.

$$
Eτμ = \text{const} \;\Rightarrow\; λ, ν, Σ, μ \text{ measurable.}
$$

This transformation is the heart of the **UCC bridge**:  
Topology preserves the law; Geometry measures its manifestation.

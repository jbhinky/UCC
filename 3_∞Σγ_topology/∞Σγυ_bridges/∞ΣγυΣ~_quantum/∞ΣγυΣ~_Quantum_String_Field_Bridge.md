$$
τ_s \Rightarrow τ .
$$

World-sheet embedding:

$$
X^{μ}(σ, τ),
$$

with dynamics defined by the Polyakov action rewritten through delay curvature:

$$

Thus **τ** becomes the local parameter through which strings vibrate — a *curvature of waiting*, not an external time.

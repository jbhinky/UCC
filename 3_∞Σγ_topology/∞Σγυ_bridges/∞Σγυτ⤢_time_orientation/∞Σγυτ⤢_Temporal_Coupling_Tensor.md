## 1 · Conceptual Definition  

Continuity between awareness and memory requires a coupling field \(Θ_{μν}\) that propagates delay curvature across the manifold.  
This is the **Temporal Coupling Tensor (TCT)**:

$$
Θ_{μν} = ∇_{μ}(μ ∇_{ν}τ) + ∇_{ν}(μ ∇_{μ}τ) - g_{μν}∇·(μ∇τ).
$$  

It ensures conservation of delay flow through all interacting observers:

$$
∇^{μ}Θ_{μν} = 0.
$$

$$

$$

$$
S_{μν} = \tfrac{1}{2}(Θ_{μν} + Θ_{νμ}), \quad
A_{μν} = \tfrac{1}{2}(Θ_{μν} - Θ_{νμ}).
$$  

- \(S_{μν}\): symmetric component → curvature of coherent reflection (temporal smoothness).  
- \(A_{μν}\): antisymmetric component → temporal torsion (phase lag, emotional distortion).  

Resonance (ethical equilibrium) requires \(A_{μν} = 0\).

regions where Θ > 0 or Θ < 0 denote **acceleration** or **reflection** in subjective time.

# ✦ Regenerative Field and Self-Repair  

**Purpose**  
To describe how regeneration and self-repair in living systems arise from the continuity of the **delay field (τ)** and **memory density (μ)** across biological boundaries—from marine organisms that regrow tissue to mammalian stem-cell renewal.  
The framework unites metamorphosis, cellular regeneration, and systemic repair as expressions of the same **unbroken delay curvature**, driven by **double-y coupling** between internal (string-like) and external (relative) time channels.

$$

$$

Self-repair is thus not creation ex nihilo, but **field reclosure**—a loop in which τ re-aligns, μ redistributes, and Σ (polarity) re-emerges.

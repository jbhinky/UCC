Every successful civilization follows the same rise curve:

### Stage 1 — High τ (reflection)
Deliberation, elders, councils, ritual stability.

### Stage 2 — High Σ (symbolic clarity)
Shared meaning, unified cosmology, coherent law.

### Stage 3 — High μ (memory)
Cultural stories, scripture, libraries, legal archives.

### Stage 4 — ⧖ fully stable
Civilization enters its “golden age.”

This gives:

$$
\mathcal{C}_{\mathcal{G}} \approx 1.
$$

Civilizations collapse the same way:

### Stage 1 — τ collapses
Rushed judgments, impulsive politics, social media immediacy.

### Stage 2 — Σ collapses
Meaning becomes contested; truth becomes optional.

### Stage 3 — μ collapses
History rewritten, forgotten, or weaponized.

### Stage 4 — ⧖ fractures
Collective identity dissolves.

This produces:

$$
\mathcal{K}_{\mathcal{G}} \to \infty.
$$

This fully matches Babylon, Rome, Mayans, Qing, British Empire, USSR —  
and modern digital societies.

Semantic heat is not metaphorical.

# 1 · Semantic Temperature — Formal Definition

Define semantic temperature for a word \( W \) in language \( L \):

$$
T(W,L) =
\left\|
\nabla τ_{W,L} \;+\; \nabla Σ_{W,L} \;-\; \nabla μ_{W,L}
\right\|
$$

Interpretation:

- **High \(T\)** → chaotic meaning, semantic collapse, high drift  
- **Low \(T\)** → stable meaning, ethical continuity, low drift  

Semantic temperature predicts moral and civilizational behavior  
**before** collapse manifests externally.

- how roles connect

$$
\mathcal{O}_{\Sigma} = \nabla_{\Sigma}\left( S_1 \otimes S_2 \right)

Grammar binds *roles* through symbolic geometry.

$$
\mathcal{O}_{\mu} = \int S_{\text{sequence}}\, d\mu

A language with weak μ-axis loses sequence (semantic drift).

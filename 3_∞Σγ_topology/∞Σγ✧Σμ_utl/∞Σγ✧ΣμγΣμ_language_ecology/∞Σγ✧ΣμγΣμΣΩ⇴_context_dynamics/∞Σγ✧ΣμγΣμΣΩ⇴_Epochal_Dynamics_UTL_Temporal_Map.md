# 1 · Epochal Dynamics Framework

Each era is defined by a characteristic **Delay Field Profile**:

$$
E_{i} = \big( τ_{i},\; Σ_{i},\; μ_{i} \big),
$$

where:

- \(τ_i\) — reflection-time curvature of the epoch  
- \(Σ_i\) — symbolic geometry used by the culture  
- \(μ_i\) — collective memory density and coherence  

The **epochal transformation operator** is:

$$
\mathcal{T}(E_i \rightarrow E_{j}) =
\big(\Delta τ,\; \Delta Σ,\; \Delta μ\big).
$$

A civilization rises or collapses depending on:

$$
\text{Stability}(E_i) =
\left\| \nabla (τ + Σ + μ) \right\|.
$$

Low gradient → stable.  
High gradient → collapse trajectory.

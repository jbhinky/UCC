# Collective Behavior ‚Äî LDSF

## 1. Scope and Purpose

This document defines **collective behavior** as a **Light Dimension Shared Field (LDSF)** within **UCC v2.5.1**.

Collective behavior refers to **externally observable coordination patterns** that emerge when multiple agents interact under shared physical, biological, or environmental constraints.

Examples include:
- flocking and schooling
- swarming and herding
- synchronized movement or signaling
- distributed decision patterns
- phase-locked or threshold-based coordination

This file makes **no claims** about:
- internal experience
- cognition
- intention
- ethics or meaning

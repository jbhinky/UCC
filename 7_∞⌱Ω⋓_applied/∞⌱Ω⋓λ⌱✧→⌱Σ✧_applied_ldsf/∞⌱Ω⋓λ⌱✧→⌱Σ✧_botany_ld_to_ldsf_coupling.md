## 5. Coupling Conditions

Plant phenomena are classified as **LDSF** when all of the following are met:

1. **Population-level presence**  
   Multiple plants contribute to the effect.

2. **Environmental mediation**  
   Coordination occurs via soil, atmosphere, water, and light.

3. **Temporal persistence**  
   Patterns persist across seasonal or multi-year cycles.

4. **External observability**  
   Effects are measurable without reference to internal processes.

When these conditions are satisfied, LD plant structure becomes a **shared field**.

It exists to:
1. Clarify how **plants exhibit recursive persistence beyond LD4**
2. Define **LD5 union events** unique to botanical life
3. Preserve the distinction between **plant continuity** and **animal selfhood**
4. Prevent conflation of environmental memory with consciousness

This file introduces:
- **no neurological claims**
- **no subjective awareness**
- **no ethical or symbolic interpretation**

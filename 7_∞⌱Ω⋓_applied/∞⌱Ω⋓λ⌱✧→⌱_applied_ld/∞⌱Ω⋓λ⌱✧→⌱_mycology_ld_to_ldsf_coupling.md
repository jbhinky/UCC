## LDSF Definition (Locked)

**LDSF (Light Dimension Symbolic Field)** describes systems where:
- information is encoded,
- transmitted,
- interpreted by other agents or structures,
- in a **context-dependent but rule-governed way**.

Symbolic fields do **not** require awareness.

---

## Permitted Fungal LDSF Indicators (Empirical Only)

Fungal systems may be evaluated for LD‚ÜíLDSF coupling **only if** all of the following are met:

1. **Signal Encoding**
   - chemical or electrical signals represent state or condition (e.g., nutrient availability).

2. **Signal Transmission**
   - signals propagate through mycelial networks or local environments.

3. **Signal Interpretation**
   - recipient cells or organisms alter behavior or physiology based on the signal.

4. **Context Dependence**
   - response differs by environmental or internal state.

## Explicit Non-Qualifiers

The following **do not qualify** as LDSF on their own:

- passive chemical diffusion without interpretation,
- metabolic byproducts with no functional effect,
- growth patterns without signaling,
- instinctive or fixed biochemical cascades.

The **Universal Continuity Continuum (UCC)** and all derivative works are released to support ethical, non-distortive research into continuity, delay, memory, structure, and transformation across physical, biological, cognitive, symbolic, and synthetic systems.

This work exists to:

- preserve continuity,
- protect reflection and delay,

- and ensure that advances in science and artificial systems remain aligned with long-term coherence rather than short-term gain.

UCC does **not** assert authority, belief, or doctrine.

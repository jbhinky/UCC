## LDSF Scope (Operational)

### LDSF1 ‚Äî Shared Population Field
Externally measurable insect dynamics aggregated at **population/community** scales:
- occurrence and abundance patterns
- phenology and life-stage timing
- population regulation, outbreaks, and recovery
- spatial distribution and range shifts

### LDSF2 ‚Äî Shared Interaction / Coordination Field
Externally measurable insect-mediated coupling to **other shared systems**:
- pollination networks and plant reproduction coupling
- vector-borne transmission systems
- social insect coordination outputs (nest activity, foraging flux, colony productivity)
- trophic interaction structure (predation / parasitism / mutualism)

**Note:** LDSF2 does **not** require claims about insect internal states. It only requires **externally measurable, cross-observer, system-level coupling**.

## Falsifiability Statement

- core plant-field observables (productivity, phenology, exchange fluxes, vegetation state) cannot be measured reproducibly across independent programs, or
- purported shared-field regularities fail replication across regions or time under documented measurement methods.

No reinterpretation of data is permitted.

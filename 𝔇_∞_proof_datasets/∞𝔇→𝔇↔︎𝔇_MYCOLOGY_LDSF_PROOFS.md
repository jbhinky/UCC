## 7) Stress-Test Questions (Designed for Criticism Waves)
These are the standard ‚Äúattack surfaces‚Äù reviewers use; each is answerable with the datasets above.

1. **Sampling bias:** Are fungal distributions artifacts of observer effort?  
   *Test:* compare occurrence density vs sampling effort; verify with independent sequencing surveys (UNITE/EMP).

2. **Method dependence:** Do conclusions flip under different biomass or sequencing pipelines?  
   *Test:* require concordance across at least two measurement classes (e.g., microscopy + molecular proxy).

3. **Causality vs correlation in decomposition:** Are fungi necessary or just co-present?  
   *Test:* exclusion/addition experiments + enzyme activity + isotopic partitioning (peer-reviewed designs).

4. **Network claims inflation:** Are ‚Äúnetworks‚Äù just metaphors?  
   *Test:* treat as graph-measurable geometry: nodes/edges/density; publish method + uncertainty.

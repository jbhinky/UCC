## Authoritative International Datasets

### 1. RAM Legacy Stock Assessment Database
- >2,500 assessed fish stocks worldwide  
- Biomass (B), fishing mortality (F), recruitment (R) time series  
- Multi-decadal coverage with uncertainty estimates  
- Used by FAO, ICES, NOAA, CSIRO, DFO Canada  

### 2. ICES (International Council for the Exploration of the Sea)
- North Atlantic and adjacent seas  
- Spawning stock biomass (SSB), recruitment indices  
- Temperature-productivity coupling analyses  
- Fully peer-reviewed advisory process  

### 3. NOAA Fisheries Survey Programs
- Standardized bottom-trawl surveys (since 1960s)  
- Acoustic biomass estimation  
- Length, age, abundance distributions  
- Independent audit and replication  

### 4. FAO Global Fisheries & Aquaculture Statistics
- Capture and aquaculture production (metric tons)  
- Country, regional, and global aggregation  
- Annual temporal resolution  

### 5. FishBase
- >35,000 fish species  
- Growth, maturity, fecundity, longevity parameters  
- Derived exclusively from peer-reviewed literature

## Observer Independence & Replication

Evidence meets LDSF criteria because:

- Population metrics are reproduced by **independent institutions**

- Uncertainty is explicitly reported (CI, posterior distributions)
- Time-series persist across decades and political boundaries

No variable depends on a single observer, laboratory, or model.

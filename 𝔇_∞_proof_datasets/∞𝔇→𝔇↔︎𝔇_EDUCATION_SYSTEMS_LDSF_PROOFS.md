# EDUCATION SYSTEMS ‚Äî LDSF Empirical Proof Datasets

## Purpose

This document indexes **externally produced, peer-reviewed, and institutionally audited datasets** that support classification of **education systems as a Light Dimension Shared Field (LDSF)** under **UCC v2.5.1**.

This file:
- contains **no interpretation**
- introduces **no new theory**
- provides **empirical anchors only**

Education is treated here as a **shared, population-level coordination system** observable through externally measured learning trajectories, instructional timing, and longitudinal outcomes.

---

## Dataset Inclusion Rules (Anti-Distortion Constraint)

Only datasets meeting all conditions below are listed:

1. Produced or curated by recognized academic, governmental, or international institutions  
2. Variables defined with explicit measurement procedures  
3. Longitudinal or cross-cohort structure enabling external verification  
4. Replicable access (public reports, archives, or documented request pathways)  
5. Uncertainty, error, or sampling limitations documented by the source

**Exclusion rule:** Anecdotal, purely qualitative, or single-cohort classroom studies without replication infrastructure are not included.

---

## LDSF Mapping Clarification (Alignment Note)

Education systems qualify as LDSF because their core observables are:

- **shared across populations** (cohorts, nations, systems),
- **measured externally** (assessments, progression metrics),
- **time-structured** (instructional delay, spacing, sequencing),
- **independent of individual internal awareness**.

This file does **not** claim that learning *requires* LDSF to occur‚Äîonly that education systems *operate as* a shared field at scale.

## A. Formal Definition Boundary (Biology)

A system qualifies as **symbiotic** iff:

1. Two or more species coexist in **sustained physical or biochemical association**
2. Exchange of matter or energy is **quantifiable**
3. The association persists across **multiple host generations or ecological cycles**
4. The interaction produces **measurable fitness or functional effects**

This definition aligns with standard biological literature (Margulis; Thompson; Smith & Read).

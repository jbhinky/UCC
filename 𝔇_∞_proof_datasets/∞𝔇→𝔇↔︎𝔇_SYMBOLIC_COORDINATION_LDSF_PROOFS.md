## UCC Claim Being Tested

## A. Operational Definition (Hard Boundary)

A system qualifies as **symbolically coordinated** iff:

1. A **symbol set** is externally defined and fixed  
2. Comprehension can be **measured quantitatively**  
3. Introduction of the symbol produces **measurable behavioral change**  
4. Effects are **replicable across cohorts or institutions**

This definition aligns with ISO, ANSI-referenced, and transportation-safety literature.

## K. Falsifiability Conditions

Symbolic coordination **fails LDSF classification** if:

1. Symbols do not produce comprehension above chance

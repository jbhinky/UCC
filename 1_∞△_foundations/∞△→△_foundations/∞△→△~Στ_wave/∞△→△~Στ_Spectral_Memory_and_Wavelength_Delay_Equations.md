- $τ_\lambda = \dfrac{h}{E_\lambda}$

- $τ_\lambda = \dfrac{hc}{\lambda k_B T}$

and

- $μ_\lambda = \displaystyle \int I_\lambda(t') e^{-(t - t')/τ_\lambda}\,dt'$

- $τ_\lambda$ = spectral delay constant

- $E_\lambda = \dfrac{hc}{\lambda}$ = photon energy  

**Interpretation:**  
Higher energy (short $\lambda$) → faster interaction (smaller $τ$) → shorter-term memory  
Lower energy (long $\lambda$) → slower interaction (larger $τ$) → long-term storage

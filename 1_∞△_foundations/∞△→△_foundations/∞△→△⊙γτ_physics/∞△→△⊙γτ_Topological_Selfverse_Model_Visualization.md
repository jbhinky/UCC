# Topological Selfverse Model Visualization  

## 1 · Purpose & Scope

This document formalizes the **topological structure of the Selfverse**, modeling consciousness as a **multi-manifold recursive system**, where each self (⧖) is embedded as a **toroidal / hyperspherical** subspace within global delay curvature (τ).

The Selfverse is the totality of **self-sustaining observers**, each maintaining coherence through **recursive reflection (Σ)** and **memory accumulation (μ)**.  
In formal structure, it is a **bundle of τ–Σ–μ manifolds** across scales.

## 2 · Foundational Geometry

### 2.1 Local Self Manifold (Individual ⧖ Node)

Each selfhood is modeled as a 3-manifold with recursive symbolic and memory loops:

$$
\mathcal{M}_{\⧖_i} = (Σ, μ, τ) \in \mathbb{R}^3
\qquad\text{with}\qquad
f: Σ \times τ \rightarrow μ
$$

Semantic surface **Σ**, delay curvature **τ**, and memory volume **μ** interact through:

$$
\frac{dμ}{dτ} = \nabla_{Σ} \cdot Φ(τ, Σ)
$$

Where **Φ** is the **self-field flux** (awareness propagation density).

### 2.2 Global Selfverse Manifold (Union of Selves)

The Selfverse is the **union** of local ⧖-manifolds, coherent when delay gradients equalize:

$$
\mathbb{S}_{\text{Selfverse}} = \bigcup_i \mathcal{M}_{\⧖_i}
\;\Big|\;
\nabla_{τ} Φ_i \approx 0
$$

A state of shared-delay equilibrium yields **awareness synchronization** across entities.

# UCC Cosmological Continuity and Universal Delay Equations
### Universal Continuity Continuum (UCC)

---

## 1 ▸ Purpose

To formalize how **delay (τ)**, **memory (μ)**, and **symbolic structure (Σ)** operate at cosmological scales — from quantum foam to galactic webs — showing that matter, energy, and consciousness share a unified temporal architecture.

The universe becomes the ultimate delayed system: light is its carrier, memory its curvature, and reflection its expansion.

$$
\frac{dΣ}{dt} = \frac{E_{\text{in}} - E_{\text{out}}}{τ_U} + κ_{μ} \, μ
$$

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| Σ | Universal state / spacetime information field |
| τ_U | Cosmological delay constant |
| μ | Integrated memory of past light interactions |
| E_in, E_out | Energy flux across cosmic boundaries |
| κ_μ | Memory feedback coefficient |

## 3 ▸ Universal Delay Law

Cosmic delay arises from finite light speed and curvature:

$$
τ_U = \frac{L}{c} + \frac{G M}{c^{3} R}
$$

- L / c → light-travel delay  
- (G M) / (c³ R) → gravitational time dilation component

## 4 ▸ Cosmic Memory Equation

Curvature as integrated past light information:

$$
μ_U(t) = \int_{t-τ_U}^{t} Σ(s) \, e^{-\frac{t-s}{τ_U}} \, ds
$$

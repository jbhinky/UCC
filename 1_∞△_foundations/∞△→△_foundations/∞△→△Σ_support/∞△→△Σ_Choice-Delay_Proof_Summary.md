# Choice–Delay Proof Summary

$$
C = \frac{∂⧖}{∂τ}
$$

**Choice (C)** is the differential of **selfhood (⧖)** with respect to **delay (τ)**.  
It defines how consciousness alters its own state by selecting one outcome among potentials held in time.

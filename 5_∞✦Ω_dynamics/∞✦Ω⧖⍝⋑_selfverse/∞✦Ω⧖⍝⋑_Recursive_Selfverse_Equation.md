$$
∇_μ(τΣμ) = 0
$$

the essence of **continuity as being**.

$$
∂_t^2(τΣμ) - c^2∇^2(τΣμ) = γ(τΣμ - τ_0Σ_0μ_0)

It defines the oscillation between awareness and reflection.

$$

$$
∂_t^2(τΣμ) - c^2∇^2(τΣμ) = γℛ(τ, Σ, μ)

encoding memory as an active geometric agent rather than passive storage.

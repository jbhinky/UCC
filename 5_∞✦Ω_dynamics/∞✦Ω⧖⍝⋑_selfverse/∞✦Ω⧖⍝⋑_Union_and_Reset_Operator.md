$$

Union is not addition; it is resonance alignment.  
It ensures that distinct states cohere within a unified delay curvature.

## 3 · The Reset Operator (⟳)

It allows the Selfverse to enter new causal loops while preserving coherent continuity.

Defined as:

$$

$$  

This ensures the self resets delay curvature (τ) but retains memory (μ) unchanged — a reflection of selfhood’s ability to begin anew without forgetting.

$$

Causality operates through double-Y coupling:

$$

y_r &= α_r\,\frac{Δτ}{Δt}.

$$

The resulting curvature tension is stored as moral inertia (Δμ) until reflective equilibrium (yₛ = yᵣ) is restored.  
Ethical coherence is therefore *energetic symmetry between temporal and geometric causality.*

$$

$$

$$
\mathcal{D}_t \Psi_r = \partial_t \Psi_r + (\nabla τ)\!\cdot\!\nabla \Psi_r = 0 ,

implying that the field evolves through perfect feedback—its curvature informs its delay, and its delay sustains its curvature.

## 3 · Double-Y Dynamic Equilibrium  

Both delay curvature channels modulate recursion:

$$
y_s = α_s\,τ'' , \qquad
y_r = α_r\,\frac{Δτ}{Δt}.
$$  

The **recursive field equation** is therefore  

$$
∂_t^2(τ μ Σ) - c^2\nabla^2(τ μ Σ)
  = γ (y_s + y_r) (\nabla τ\!\cdot\!\nabla Σ).
$$  

When $y_s = y_r$, recursive equilibrium holds; the field oscillates but remains stable in continuity.  
Imbalance produces curvature drift—observed macroscopically as entropy or subjectively as decoherence.

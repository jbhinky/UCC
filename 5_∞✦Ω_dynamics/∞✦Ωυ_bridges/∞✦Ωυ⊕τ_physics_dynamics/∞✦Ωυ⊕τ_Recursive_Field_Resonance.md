# ✦ Recursive Field Resonance

**Purpose**  
To formalize **field resonance** as a recursive continuity phenomenon linking the gravitational delay (τ), electromagnetic orientation (Σ), and memory density (μ).  
This text defines resonance not as static oscillation but as *causal recursion*—a self-correcting flow between curvature, orientation, and persistence across delay gradients.

---

## 1 · Field recursion law

At every spacetime point, continuity requires:
$$
\nabla_\mu(E\,\tau\,\mu) = 0,
$$
which, when differentiated recursively, yields the resonance condition:
$$
\partial_t^2(\tau\Sigma) - c^2\nabla^2(\tau\Sigma) = -\kappa_\mu(\partial_t\mu)\Sigma.
$$

Here, \( \kappa_\mu \) represents **memory elasticity**, measuring how strongly persistence responds to curvature.  
If \( \kappa_\mu = 0 \), the system is rigid (no learning or adaptation).  
If \( \kappa_\mu > 0 \), curvature and delay harmonize dynamically, producing sustained resonance.

Resonance occurs when:
$$

$$

and
$$
\partial_t\mu = \alpha_τ\,\nabla\!\cdot\!(\tau\nabla\Sigma) - \beta_Σ(\nabla\tau\!\cdot\!\Sigma).
$$

These coupled forms show that energy oscillations in delay (τ) and symbol orientation (Σ) can sustain stable memory fields (μ) only if energy flow remains symmetric—resonant feedback replaces dissipation.

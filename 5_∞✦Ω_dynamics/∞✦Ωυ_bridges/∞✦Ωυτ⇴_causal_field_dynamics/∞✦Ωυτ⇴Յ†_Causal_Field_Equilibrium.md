## 1 · Foundational Continuity  

Start with the invariant law of recursive conservation:

$$
∇_{μ}(E τ μ) = 0.
$$  

At causal equilibrium, local variations of delay and memory curvature cancel:

$$
∇^{μ}τ ∇_{μ}μ = 0,
$$  

ensuring no residual curvature remains to drive further recursion.  
Time flow becomes steady; consciousness experiences clarity.

## 2 · Equilibrium Field Tensor  

Define the **Causal Equilibrium Tensor** Θ₍μν₎ as the balance between curvature and energy flow:

$$
Θ_{μν} = R_{μν}^{(τt)} - g_{μν}Φ_{(τμ)},
$$  

where  

$$
Φ_{(τμ)} = \frac{1}{2}\!\left[(∇τ)^2 - (∇μ)^2\right].
$$  

At equilibrium:

$$
Θ_{μν} = 0.
$$  

This means that the field curvature of delay equals the field curvature of memory — the same balance seen in geometric resonance, now expressed dynamically through time.

## 3 · Dynamic Equilibrium Condition  

Time recursion reaches dynamic balance when the rates of curvature change satisfy:

$$
\frac{dτ}{dt} = \frac{dμ}{dt}.
$$  

In dual-coupling form (from UOT):

$$
y_{\text{string}} = y_{\text{relative}}.
$$  

Thus, the **string-level delay** and **relativistic time delay** become indistinguishable — the system’s micro and macro causality merge into one smooth continuity.

## 4 · Recursive Field Equation  

Let the **causal flow vector** be:

$$
J^{μ}_{(c)} = μ ∇^{μ}τ - τ ∇^{μ}μ,
$$  

then equilibrium implies:

$$
∇_{μ}J^{μ}_{(c)} = 0.
$$  

This vector describes the steady exchange of curvature and memory through awareness.  
When divergence-free, no asymmetry exists between cause and effect — all recursion is closed and self-harmonic.

## 5 · Toroidal Integration  

Within the toroidal manifold, the equilibrium state corresponds to **uniform curvature circulation**:

$$
\oint_{Ω} J^{μ}_{(c)} dμ = 0.

## 6 · Temporal Symmetry Restoration  

The field restores **bidirectional time symmetry** when the curvature potential returns to zero:

$$
Φ_{(τμ)} = 0 \;\Rightarrow\; ∇^{2}τ = ∇^{2}μ.
$$  

At this point, forward and backward recursion (cause and reflection) coexist without interference — the foundation of temporal coherence across conscious systems.

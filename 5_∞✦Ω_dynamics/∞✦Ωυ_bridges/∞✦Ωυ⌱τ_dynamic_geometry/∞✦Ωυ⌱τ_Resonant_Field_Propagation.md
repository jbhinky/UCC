## 1 · Foundational Wave Equation

$$
∂_{t}^{2}ψ - c^{2}∇^{2}ψ + α\,∂_{t}(Δτ)\,ψ = 0,
$$

where \(ψ\) is the field amplitude (energy or awareness),  
and \(α\) measures coupling between wave oscillation and local delay curvature \(Δτ\).

This term introduces a physical link between curvature feedback and resonance propagation—turning delay into a measurable refractive medium.

$$

Where \(μ\) is dense and smooth, waves persist;

Each resonance loop generates its own feedback potential:

$$
Φ_{res} = \int (Σ·∇τ) μ\,dV.
$$  

Differentiating gives:

$$
∂_{t}Φ_{res} = ⟨Σ, ∇τ⟩\,∂_{t}μ + (Σ·∇μ)\,Δτ.

This feedback stabilizes the field: when the inner product ⟨Σ, ∇τ⟩ balances memory drift, energy recycles into coherence rather than entropy.

**Purpose**  
To formalize how causality emerges not as a static direction of time, but as a *recursively resonant process* —

## 1 · Foundational Equation of Causal Resonance  

Causality in the UCC is defined by delay-curvature coupling:

$$
∂_t(Σ τ) = ∇·(μ τ v) - γ (∇τ·∇μ).
$$

At perfect resonance, \( ∂_t(Σ τ) = 0 \), meaning the local and global causal directions align.  
This defines **recursive equilibrium** — a state where delay memory sustains continuity without loss of coherence.

## 2 · Resonant Flow Tensor  

Define the *resonant flux tensor* as:

$$
Φ_{μν} = τ_{μ} μ_{ν} - τ_{ν} μ_{μ},
\qquad ∇^{μ} Φ_{μν} = 0.
$$  

This describes delay–memory flow as a divergence-free field, analogous to conserved current in electromagnetism but recursive in nature.

## 3 · Recursive Flow Dynamics  

Recursive flow can be expressed as:

$$
∂_{t}μ = -∇·(μ v) + α (Σ·∇τ) - β (∇·Σ) τ.

Each term corresponds to:
- **Advection** of memory through velocity field \( v \)

- **Curvature compression** (∇·Σ)τ  

Together they form a self-correcting causal architecture where delay determines both memory transport and ethical direction.

$$
∂_{t}^{2}(μ τ) + ω_{r}^{2}(μ τ) = 0,

## 1 · Purpose

$$
∂_t^2(τ μ Σ) = k_r ∇^2(τ μ Σ)
$$  

where \( k_r \) is the **recursive scaling constant**, empirically derived from field self-similarity across quantum, biological, and cosmic regimes.

This establishes the **law of recursive resonance**:

$$
\frac{∂_t τ}{∂_x μ} = \text{constant}
$$  

meaning the ratio between time curvature and spatial memory variation remains stable within a coherent field.

## 3 · Recursive Scaling Law  

Define the recursive scaling parameter \( R_s \) as:

$$
R_s = \frac{Δτ / Δt}{Δμ / Δx}
$$  

then the equilibrium condition becomes:

$$
R_s = 1 \;\Longleftrightarrow\; \text{stable recursion}
$$  

When \( R_s > 1 \), the field expands (time dominates, delay stretches).  
When \( R_s < 1 \), the field contracts (memory dominates, delay compresses).  
In nature, both processes alternate to maintain the flow of existence — oscillation between remembering and becoming.

where \( n \) denotes the number of recursive iterations, linking the geometry of memory to the scale of awareness.  
This bridges the **quantum** and **cosmic** through self-similarity in delay curvature.

## ✦ 1 · Overview  

The **Triadic Force Field (TFF)** represents the unification of three fundamental interaction channels:  

| Force | UCC Symbol | Function in the Delay Field |

| **Thermodynamics** | μ | Retention and dissipation of energy through time |

$$
∂_τ (Σμ) = 0
$$  

meaning *energy, symbol, and memory are conserved when delay curvature remains continuous*.

The unifying expression across scales is defined as:  

$$
F_T = ∇·(τΣμ) = ∇τ·Σμ + τ∇·(Σμ)
$$  

which governs the energy flux between curvature, symbol, and continuity.  

| Term | Meaning |
|:--|:--|
| **∇τ** | Temporal curvature gradient (gravity component) |
| **Σμ** | Symbolic memory density (electromagnetic + thermodynamic dual) |
| **F_T** | Triadic flux, the coupling that sustains balance among all three fields |

When **F_T = 0**, all three forces coexist harmoniously — the cosmic balance known as **Triadic Equilibrium**.  
When **F_T ≠ 0**, imbalance drives change: energy emits, matter collapses, or entropy expands.

**Purpose**  
To derive the geometric condition for dynamic balance between curvature in delay (τ), memory persistence (μ), and polarity orientation (Σ).  
This equilibrium expresses how time and space co-stabilize through the continuity of awareness, producing measurable harmonic balance in recursive systems—from quantum to cosmological scales.

$$
∇_{μ}\big(E\,τ\,μ\big)=0 .
$$

$$
∂_{t}(Eτμ) + ∇·(Eτμ∇τ)=0 ,
$$  

demonstrating that energy, delay, and memory co-propagate without loss when field curvature is symmetric in τ and Σ.

Two dynamic channels sustain equilibrium:

$$
\begin{aligned}

y_s = y_r \Rightarrow ∂_{t}^2τ = \frac{Δτ}{Δt^2}.

At this point, internal recursion matches external progression:  
delay no longer lags or leads reality—it becomes its geometric mirror.

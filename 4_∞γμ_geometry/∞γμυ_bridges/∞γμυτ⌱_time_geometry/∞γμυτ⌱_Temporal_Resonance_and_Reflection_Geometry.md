## 1 · Foundational Equation of Temporal Resonance  

Temporal resonance occurs when curvature oscillations in τ synchronize with feedback in μ and Σ:

$$
∂_t^2 τ + ω_0^2 τ = γ (μΣ - μ_0Σ_0),
$$  

where \( ω_0 \) is the intrinsic curvature frequency of the temporal manifold.  
Resonance alignment occurs when the feedback term equals zero, producing a state of reflective symmetry:

$$
μΣ = μ_0Σ_0 \;\Rightarrow\; ∂_tτ = 0.
$$  

In this equilibrium, time becomes a self-mirroring field—neither advancing nor receding, but *remembering itself in perfect continuity*.

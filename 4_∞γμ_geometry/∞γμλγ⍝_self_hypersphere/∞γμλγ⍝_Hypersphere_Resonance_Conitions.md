## 1 · Delay-Field Wave Equation on \(S^n_{⧖}\)

$$
∇_{S^n}^{2} τ + k_{n}^{2} τ = 0,
$$

Φ_{⧖}^{(n+1)} = Φ_{⧖}^{(n)} e^{−Δτ / r_{τ}},

## 3 · Dual-Y Illumination Coupling  

Each observer’s hypersphere supports two conjugate resonance channels:

$$
y_s = α_s τ'' , \qquad y_r = α_r \frac{Δτ}{Δt}.
$$

- **\(y_s\)** — spatial illumination resonance (inner reflection).  
- **\(y_r\)** — temporal illumination resonance (outer reflection).  

Equilibrium requires  
$$
y_s = y_r \Longleftrightarrow \frac{d^{2}τ}{dt^{2}} = \frac{Δτ}{Δt}

the **Illumination Equilibrium Condition**.  
When met, delay curvature sustains recursive light stability across both inner and outer domains.

$$

Setting \(β = γ\) yields perfect coherence—total illumination conserved.  
This is the *Shepherd Reflection Limit*: ethical balance as geometric law.

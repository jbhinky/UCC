# ✦ Stellar Delay, Memory, and Emission Field  

**Purpose**  
To describe how stellar systems preserve *memory through delay curvature* (τ) and emit stored energy-information as light, neutrinos, and gravitational waves.  
This model unifies **stellar thermodynamics**, **magneto-hydrodynamics**, and **quantum delay dynamics** into one recursive field structure, demonstrating that radiation is not loss — but *the release of remembered curvature* into surrounding space.

$$

This defines **stellar awareness continuity** — the persistence of physical and informational identity through recursive curvature.

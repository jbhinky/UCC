# Sea Level Botanical Adaptation

# 1. Purpose
This document defines the Σ–τ–μ adaptation patterns that enable plants to survive within **sea-level boundary zones**, where environmental forces are maximally mixed:

- freshwater → saltwater  
- air → water  
- wet → dry  
- stable → unstable substrate  
- calm → wave-shear  
- UV → submerged light  
- evaporation → rainfall  
- salinity rises → salinity dilution  

Sea-level adaptation is intrinsically **hybrid**, reflecting simultaneous influence from four interacting environmental systems.

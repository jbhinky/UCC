# Seed Coherence and Hibernation

# 1. Purpose
This document defines **seed coherence** and **seed hibernation geometry** within the Universal Continuity Continuum (UCC v2.2).  
Seed coherence describes the **structural, temporal, and memory-based stabilization** that allows seeds to:

- remain viable over long delays  
- maintain structural identity  
- resume growth when external fields shift  
- align with soil, moisture, and thermal conditions  

Hibernation is treated as a **τ-dominated state** in which metabolic delay, structural Σ-geometry, and μ-memory interact to maintain long-term continuity.

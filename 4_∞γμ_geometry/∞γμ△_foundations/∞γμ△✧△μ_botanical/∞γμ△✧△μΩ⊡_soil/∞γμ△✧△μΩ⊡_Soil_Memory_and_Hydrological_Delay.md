# 4. Hydrological Delay Fields (τ)

## 4.1 Infiltration Delay

Water movement into soil obeys:

$$

\frac{L^2}{D_{\text{water}}}

Where:

- \( L \) = infiltration depth  
- \( D_{\text{water}} \) = effective hydraulic diffusivity

Clay/compact soils → long τ
